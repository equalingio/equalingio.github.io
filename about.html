Who are we?

Equaलिंग is a student-run feminist initiative in India which aims at creating an atmosphere of equality and safety for all the genders through educating the young,impressionable minds that will shape the future of the country. This recent initiative of 2020 has been found by Ms. Anushka Mathur,a law aspirant and Mr. Tanmay Mathur, a computer science engineer in-making, with a vision to transform the present societal structure and norms by spreading awareness and initiating conversations about the largely neglected hurdles to equality and freedom. It has members and volunteers spread all across the nation, passionate about changing the discriminatory and toxic mindset of the society.

Our Objectives

  1. * To challenge the patriarchal and discriminatory notions prevalent in the society

  2. * To combat rape culture and the associated social habits of slut-shaming, victim-blaming and victim-shaming

  3. * To neutralize the gender roles assigned by society at the very outset of one’s life.

  4. * To normalize the non-conformist behavior of individuals towards the established gender roles, sometimes termed as “Deviant behaviour”.

  • * To plan and execute Campaigns for school students to make them aware of the brunts of harsh/toxic Masculinity and patriarchy faced by society at large.

  • * To counter the stigmas attached to any particular gender and to ensure that everyone has the right to express themselves

  • * To promote equal treatment of all irrespective of their gender
