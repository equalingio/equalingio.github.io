Volunteer Programme

Equaलिंग Volunteer Programme forms the core of the initiative and aims at mustering in like-minded people from across the nation. Our team realises the importance of establishing a network of passionate and driven individuals motivated to devote a part of their time for our cause. As our focus remains on bringing ideological and fundamental changes in the society, we believe it is imperative that we bring together the scattered voices at a common platform and do our best to amplify them so as to aware and transform as many people as we can.


Work

As a volunteer of Equaलिंग, you shall be expected to work online or at ground level (depending on the circumstances and keeping in mind everybody’s safety) for one of the following teams:
• Content Team
• Social media Team
• Podcast Team
• PR and Marketing Team
• Graphic Designing Team
• Video Editing Team
• Campaign Team
• Or any other team formed as per requirements
You shall also be able to participate in the various events and activities organised by the Equaलिंग Team, free of cost.


Application

Interested candidates can apply at Equaलिंग by mailing us your application at equaling.volunteer@yahoo.com. The application must contain a cover letter and an updated curriculum vitae attached. The subject of the mail must be ‘Application for Equaलिंग Volunteer Programme’.


Collaborations/Affiliations/Other Queries

For any collaboration/affiliation proposal or other forms of query, you may contact us at equaling.org@gmail.com.
You may also share your stories/incidents, or communicate/seek any kind of assistance, by mailing us at the same address.
